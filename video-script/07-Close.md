## Audio Considerations

### Music Selection
- **Genre:** Cinematic, inspirational (Hans Zimmer style)
- **Tempo:** Building to climax, then gentle resolution
- **Volume:** Subtle background, never overpowers voice
- **Licensing:** Ensure royalty-free (YouTube Audio Library, Epidemic Sound)

### Voiceover Delivery
- **Pace:** Confident but not rushed
- **Emotion:** Inspiring, hopeful, determined
- **Energy:** High but controlled (not shouty)
- **Ending:** Warm, inviting ("Let's build this together")
