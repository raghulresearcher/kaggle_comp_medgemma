## Impact Credibility Factors

**Why Judges Will Believe These Numbers:**

1. ✅ **Conservative Estimate:** 15% improvement (studies show 20-50%)
2. ✅ **Established Baseline:** $300B non-adherence cost (NIH data)
3. ✅ **Clear Calculation:** 125K deaths × 15% improvement = 18,750
4. ✅ **Phased Scaling:** Not claiming instant national deployment
5. ✅ **Economic Model:** $5-10/month cost, $450/month value = 45:1 ROI
